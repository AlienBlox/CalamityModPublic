This mod was created with the purpose of adding additional content to Terraria.
It currently contains:
8 new mineable ores
24 new bosses
4 new modes for extra challenge
3 new biomes
275+ new enemy NPCs
1400+ new items
Cross-content support for GRealm
PLEASE BE ADVISED: Download the Calamity Mod Music mod if you want Calamity Mod music to be in your game!
